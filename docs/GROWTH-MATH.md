# Glide — Growth Math (Kevin's revenue targets, July 2026)

_Kevin's question (Jul 8, 2026): how do I get MYSELF to ~$500K this year as a solo trainer
($75–100/session today), what trainer/client combinations reach $1M → $1B/yr on the decided
pricing menu, and how does the app get to 10M–100M users? Companion to docs/PRICING.md (unit
costs + the decided tier menu) and docs/ECOSYSTEM.md (platform vision)._

## 1. Kevin solo → $500K/yr run-rate

**The cap being broken:** $500K at $100/session = 5,000 sessions/yr ≈ 96/week — impossible.
Hours-for-dollars tops out ~$150–200K. Glide's job is decoupling revenue from hours.

| Stream | Move | Run-rate |
|---|---|---|
| In-person | $110+ floor, packages, fewer/better sessions | 25/wk × $110 × 48 ≈ **$132K** |
| **Online/hybrid coaching via Glide** | 90–100 clients at $200–400/mo — the AI layer (logging, programs, dashboards, nudges, Trainerize sync) is what makes 90+ clients carryable by one coach | 90 × $300/mo ≈ **$324K** |
| Client subs (100% margin — Kevin owns the platform) | ~100 × $15/mo | ≈ **$18K** |
| First outside coaches | 20–30 × $49–79/mo | ≈ **$15K** |
| **Exit run-rate** | | **≈ $490K** |

Honesty: this is the DECEMBER run-rate. Cash banked in year one is realistically $250–350K
because the online-client ramp (to ~90 by mid-year) is a marketing/content grind — Glide makes
online coaching *deliverable*, not *sold*. The online-coaching line is the whole game.

## 2. Revenue-target combinations (decided menu)

Blended building blocks: paying client ≈ **$160/yr** · coach sub ≈ **$550/yr** ·
"coach unit" = 1 coach + ~15 paying clients ≈ **$2,950/yr SaaS**. With **Stripe Connect**
(roadmap: 15% platform cut of coach billings; a coach billing $4K/mo → +$7,200/yr) a coach
unit ≈ **$10K/yr** — at scale, payments out-earn subscriptions (the Shopify lesson).

| Annual revenue | Pure consumer (paying clients) | Coach-led SaaS (coaches + clients) | Coach-led + payments (coaches) |
|---|---|---|---|
| $1M | ~6,300 | ~340 + ~5,100 | ~100 |
| $10M | ~63,000 | ~3,400 + ~51,000 | ~1,000 |
| $100M | ~625,000 | ~34,000 + ~510,000 | ~10,000 |
| $500M | ~3.1M | ~170,000 + ~2.5M | ~50,000 |
| $1B | ~6.3M | ~340,000 + ~5.1M | ~100,000 |

**Reality calibration:**
- US ≈ 350–400K certified trainers total (~1M globally) → the coach-SaaS column dies past
  ~$100M. **Beyond $100M only two engines exist: consumer scale + the payments layer** (plus
  white-label gyms/studios bringing users in blocks — the original Shopify-style vision).
- MyFitnessPal: ~200M registered users, est. ~$300M/yr. **$1B/yr is bigger than today's MFP.**
- Free→paid at 3–5%: **10M users ≈ $50–80M ARR · 100M users ≈ $500–800M ARR** — "100M users"
  and "$500M+" are the same goal.
- The AI COGS hard-cap (PRICING.md) means margins hold at EVERY row — unit economics don't
  break at scale.

## 3. Path to 10M–100M users (in order of what matters)

1. **Retention before reach** — prove day-30/90 logging retention with the first few hundred
   users before buying growth; a leaky bucket wastes every acquisition dollar.
2. **Coach flywheel = the unfair advantage** — every coach recruits 10–50 clients (invite
   links / QR / referrals already live, S81/S83). An unpaid salesforce consumer apps lack.
3. **Native app-store presence is the biggest structural gap** — Glide is a PWA; nobody hits
   10M consumers without iOS/Android listings. First real build on the 10M path.
4. **Cal AI playbook for consumer volume** — paid TikTok/IG creators on per-install deals
   (~5M downloads in ~2 yrs); repeatable and budgetable, but cash-hungry.
5. **The funding fork** — 10M users needs team + capital (raise) or coach-revenue-funded
   patience (MFP/Strava each took 10–15 yrs to that territory).
6. **"Works with your AI" / MCP interop = the PR wedge** nobody in fitness has.

**Sequencing: $500K (Kevin, this year) → $1M ARR (~340 coaches, or ~100 with Connect) →
build Stripe Connect → $10M (~1,000 billing coaches) → native apps + creator engine →
consumer scale.** Each stage funds the next.
