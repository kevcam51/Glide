GLIDNA

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This policy explains what Glidna (operated by Smooth Training — "we," "us") collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. The short version: your fitness data is yours, we use it only to run Glidna, and we never sell it.

1. What we collect

  • Account info: name, email, and your role (client or trainer).
  • Fitness & health data you enter: weight, goals, body measurements, food and calorie logs, workouts, water intake, check-ins, and notes.
  • Content you send: messages with your trainer, chats with the AI assistant, and any meal photos or voice recordings you choose to share with it.
  • Connected-service data you authorize — for example, fitness-tracker stats (daily burn, steps) synced from a coaching platform your trainer uses.
  • Payment info: handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card number, only your subscription status.
  • Basic technical data: device/browser type and app activity needed to keep Glidna working and secure.

2. How we use it

  • To run Glidna: your dashboards, plans, projections, streaks, and calendars.
  • AI features: when you use the AI assistant, your message (and the relevant fitness data or photo needed to answer) is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate the response. Voice notes are transcribed by a speech-to-text provider. We use API services whose terms prohibit training their models on your data.
  • To send notifications you've enabled (reminders, messages, to-dos) — every type has a toggle in the app's Notification Center.
  • To bill subscriptions (Stripe) and send invitation emails you request (Resend).

3. Who can see your data

Your trainer, if you link one. Linking with a trainer authorizes that trainer (and their head trainer, if they work under one) to view and edit your fitness data — plans, logs, weigh-ins — and to message you. This is the point of the platform, but it's always your choice: unlinking (Home → Leave trainer) ends their access to future data. No other Glidna user can ever see your data.

Service providers that make Glidna work process data on our behalf: Google Firebase (database, sign-in), Anthropic (AI responses), Groq/OpenAI (voice transcription), Stripe (payments), Resend (invitation email), and Vercel (hosting). Each receives only what its job requires.

We do not sell your personal data, and we don't share it with advertisers. We'd disclose data only if legally compelled.

4. Security

Data is encrypted in transit; access is enforced by server-side security rules that are tested before every change (your data is readable only by you, your linked trainer chain, and our administrator). Sign-in supports passkeys (Face ID / Touch ID), which never leave your device.

5. Your choices & rights

  • Export: you can export your data from the app anytime.
  • Delete: email us to delete your account and data; we'll confirm and complete it within 30 days (an in-app deletion flow is planned).
  • Notifications: every notification type is a toggle in the app.
  • Unlink: leave your trainer at any time to end their access.

6. Retention, children, changes

We keep your data while your account exists and delete it on request. Glidna is for adults 18+. If we materially change this policy, we'll announce it in the app before it takes effect.

7. Contact

Privacy questions or deletion requests: kevin@smoothtraining.com
