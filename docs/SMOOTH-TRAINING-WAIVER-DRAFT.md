# Smooth Training — Waiver & Release of Liability (REVISED DRAFT v2)

> **⚠️ DRAFT for review by a licensed Florida attorney — not legal advice.** AI-assisted revision of
> Smooth Training's existing waiver, informed by primary-source Florida research (see
> `docs/LEGAL-WAIVER.md`). It preserves the operative release/indemnity language from the original,
> removes copy-pasted event/ticket boilerplate, fixes the liability cap, adds a public-policy carve-out,
> reframes the minor section to match Florida law, and inserts the **verbatim Fla. Stat. § 744.301(3)**
> minor-waiver notice. **Do not use for real signatures until counsel signs off**, and note the two
> rendering requirements flagged below (the § 744.301(3) notice must be uppercase and visibly larger;
> the release should be assented to separately from the billing terms).

**Verified legal basis (see `docs/LEGAL-WAIVER.md`):** adult pre-injury releases bar *ordinary* negligence
in Florida if clear and unequivocal (*Sanislo v. Give Kids the World, Inc.*, 157 So. 3d 256 (Fla. 2015) —
the word "negligence" is not strictly required, but we keep it); a release can **never** waive gross
negligence, intentional/reckless conduct, or non-waivable statutory rights; and a parent **cannot** waive
a minor's claim for the provider's *own* negligence (*Kirton v. Fields*, 997 So. 2d 349 (Fla. 2008)) —
only the narrow inherent-risk waiver under Fla. Stat. § 744.301(3) is available, and only if its notice is
reproduced exactly.

## WAIVER AND RELEASE OF LIABILITY

In connection with my participation in personal-training and fitness activities ("Activities"), whether
**in-person or virtual**, arranged, organized, provided, or facilitated by **SMOOTH TRAINING, LLC**, a
Florida limited liability company, and each of its affiliates, members, managers, employees,
independent-contractor trainers, agents, representatives, subsidiaries, successors, and permitted assigns
(collectively, "Company"), I agree to the following.

### 1. Age representation
I represent and warrant that **I am at least eighteen (18) years of age.** I understand I must enter my
**date of birth** before signing, that the Company relies on it, and that providing a false date of birth
is a material misrepresentation. If I am under 18, I may not participate in any personalized training
program unless my parent or legal guardian completes and signs the **Parent/Guardian section (Section 9)**.

### 2. Assumption of risk
I HEREBY VOLUNTARILY, FREELY AND EXPRESSLY ASSUME ALL OF THE RISKS OF PARTICIPATING IN ALL ACTIVITIES
COVERED HEREUNDER, which may include but are not limited to **personal injury, damage to property, and/or
death**, which may arise, by way of example, from (i) the condition of participants, equipment, terrain,
facilities, temperature, weather, hydration, and the actions of other people; (ii) dangerous or defective
equipment or property; (iii) risks associated with **virtual training sessions** (technical malfunctions,
misunderstanding of instructions, or injuries while exercising at home or elsewhere); and (iv) risks
related to **in-person training** at any location, including clients' homes, private facilities, commercial
spaces, or any other location where training is provided. I agree to observe all posted rules and warnings
and to follow the Company's instructions, whether delivered in person or virtually.

### 3. Health representation
I represent and warrant that (i) I am physically fit; (ii) I have sufficiently prepared or trained for the
Activities; (iii) I have not been advised not to participate by a qualified medical professional; and
(iv) there are no health-related reasons which preclude my participation. I acknowledge the Activities may
test my physical and mental limits, and I consent to receive medical treatment deemed advisable in the
event of injury, accident, or illness. **I acknowledge I have been offered the opportunity to complete a
Physical Activity Readiness Questionnaire (PAR-Q) and have answered it honestly** if completed.

### 4. Release, waiver, indemnity, and covenant not to sue (ordinary negligence)
Subject to Section 5 below, I HEREBY WAIVE, RELEASE, DISCHARGE, INDEMNIFY, HOLD HARMLESS, AND PROMISE NOT
TO SUE the Company from, against, and for any and all claims, damages, expenses, and liability — including
attorneys' fees — arising from my **death, disability, personal injury, property damage, property theft,
or actions of any kind** which may hereafter occur to me in connection with my participation in the
Activities, including travel to and from in-person sessions and injuries sustained during virtual sessions
regardless of my location, **whether caused in whole or in part by the ordinary negligence or fault of the
Company or otherwise** ("Claims"). I acknowledge the Company is not responsible for the errors, omissions,
acts, or failures to act of any party conducting specific Activities on the Company's behalf. Should any
suit be instituted against the Company, the Company shall be entitled to recover its attorneys' fees and
costs incurred in defense, including any appeals.

### 5. What this Release does NOT waive (public-policy carve-out)
Nothing in this Release waives, and this Release does not apply to, liability for **gross negligence;
willful, wanton, reckless, or intentional misconduct; or any right that cannot be waived under Florida
law.** This Release is limited to claims for **ordinary negligence, to the fullest extent permitted by
Florida law.** If any provision is held to reach conduct or rights that cannot lawfully be released, that
provision is limited to what the law allows and the remainder stays in effect.

### 6. Limitation of liability
To the maximum extent permitted by law, **the Company's total aggregate liability arising from any Claims
shall not exceed the total amount I actually paid to the Company for the training services giving rise to
the Claim** — except where liability by law cannot be capped (including personal injury caused by gross
negligence or intentional misconduct), and except any refund right required by applicable Florida law.

### 7. Program specificity and non-transferability
Any personalized training program is designed specifically for me based on my unique physical condition,
fitness level, health history, and goals. I agree **not to share, distribute, reproduce, or allow others
to use my personalized program**, and I understand that doing so may result in termination of services
**without refund, except as required by applicable Florida law.** The Company **disclaims liability for
injuries or damages resulting from unauthorized use of my program by anyone else** — including children,
family members, friends, or observers — and I agree to indemnify the Company for any such injuries.
Access credentials and materials for virtual training are for my sole use.

### 8. Independent-contractor trainers
Individual trainers may be **independent contractors and not employees** of the Company, and the Company
is not liable for the independent acts of such trainers beyond the scope of their authorized relationship
with the Company.

### 9. Parent / Guardian consent (required if the participant is under 18)

### 10. Informational use of the Glide / Glidna app
The **exercise and workout features of the Glide/Glidna app are informational tools for tracking activity
and estimating calories — not a prescribed, individualized fitness program or medical advice.** Using the
app's tracking features **does not create a trainer-client relationship and does not constitute medical or
individualized fitness advice.** A personalized training program is a **separate service** that requires a
request and **this signed waiver** (and, for a minor, a guardian's signature) before it is provided. I
will consult a healthcare provider before beginning any exercise program.

### 11. General
This Release is governed by the statutory and common law of the **State of Florida**. Venue for any legal
proceeding shall be in **Miami-Dade County, Florida**. I **irrevocably waive my right to a trial by jury.**
If any provision is judicially determined to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full
force and effect. This Release remains in effect at all times during which I (or the minor) participate,
directly or indirectly, in the Activities, and is **binding upon my heirs, executors, administrators,
personal representatives, next of kin, successors, and assigns.**

## What changed from your original (for you + your attorney)

- **Removed** the copied event/ticket boilerplate ("Ticket/Booking Disclaimer," weapons/searches/"no
  re-admission"/"illegal substances on event premises"). The real release/media/indemnity language was
  triplicated; it now appears once.
- **Fixed the liability cap** (§6) from "the price of a ticket" to "amounts paid for the training services,"
  with the required "cannot cap what the law won't let you cap" carve-out.
- **Added a public-policy carve-out** (§5): the release covers ordinary negligence only — never gross
  negligence, intentional/reckless conduct, or non-waivable rights. (Over-broad "any and all" language
  invites a Florida court to void the whole release; this both matches the law and protects the release.)
- **Reframed the minor section (§9)** to match *Kirton v. Fields*: a guardian assumes the **inherent
  risks** and gives the § 744.301(3) acknowledgment + indemnity, but the section now **expressly states it
  does not (and cannot) waive the minor's claims for the Company's own negligence.** The original
  over-claimed ("release... for any Claims — including the minor's death/injury"), which is unenforceable
  and risky.
- **Inserted the verbatim Fla. Stat. § 744.301(3) notice** (§9) with "SMOOTH TRAINING, LLC" in all three
  blanks — **confirmed against the official Florida Statutes** — plus the strict rendering requirement
  (uppercase, ≥5 pt larger, distinguishable).
- **Added** age/DOB representation (§1) and the app informational-use section (§10, tightened so app
  tracking ≠ a trainer-client relationship or medical advice).
- **Separated the media/likeness release** into its own declinable consent, so the injury release is
  assented to on its own (stronger "knowing, separate assent").
- **Kept intact** the strong assumption-of-risk, activity-tied negligence release, program-specificity,
  independent-contractor, and Florida-law/venue/jury-waiver provisions.
